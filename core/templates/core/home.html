SIMPLE PROCESS How ClearTrack Works

1

Student Registration

Administrators register students in the system or import them in batch via CSV files.

2

QR Code Generation

Each student receives a unique QR code that contains their identity information.

3

Clearance Assignment

Administrators create clearance templates and assign clearance fields to appropriate staff.

4

Scanning & Signing

Staff scan student QR codes and digitally sign their assigned clearance fields.

5

Daily Reporting

Staff submit daily reports of signed clearances for administrative review.

6

Progress Tracking

Administrators monitor clearance progress through comprehensive analytics dashboards.

7

Final Approval

Once all fields are signed, administrators provide final approval signatures.

8

Clearance Completion

Students receive completed digital clearance forms, accessible anytime.
